Website

We created this website for a nail artist who had no digital presence, so we had a free hand to create a visual identity and design we like. She needed a simple solution where she can easily show her Previous work like on Instagram. For this reason, we implemented an Instagram Iframe, so she doesn’t have to worry about updating the page.
The other important thing for her is the booking system so her clients can book easily and She doesn’t have to reply to all the messages.
